Vandal

Specializing in large-scale murals and wildstyle lettering, Vandal has been a staple in the underground scene for over a decade. His work blends neon aesthetics with gritty textures.

Ghost

Known for his monochromatic characters and elusive street pieces. Ghost focuses on the interaction between urban decay and modern illustrative clean lines.

Pink Ink

A pioneer of the spray-paint stenciling technique. Her work often features social commentary hidden behind bright, explosive bursts of color and geometric patterns.
